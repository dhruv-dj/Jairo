About Us

Jairo is an Order Relay Process, SaaS platform based out of Delhi, providing solutions for communication, productivity and business operations. Order Relay process is the smartest way to raise and process orders and maximise efficiency of your business operations. We combine traditional business approach with technology and bring you user friendly solutions.
